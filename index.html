The Best Places to Visit in Florida

List provided by U.S. News & World Report.

Based off of the U.S. News & World Report, above is the locations of the top 20 places to visit in Florida. Each location has a marker that lists its ranking from 1-20. Currently, Sarasota is ranked at the number one spot. Sarasota is known for its state parks and beautiful beaches. This list is helpful to determine what spots to visit in Florida as it is such a big state filled with vacation destinations and attractions. As you can see from the map above, you can go to pretty much any part of Florida and find a great travel destination.
